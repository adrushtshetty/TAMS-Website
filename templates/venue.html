The History of TAMS

The first edition of TAS was held in 2013. The first edition of TAM was held in 2023. Let's take a look at how far we've come.


The Amateur Scientist is an esteemed science fest fostering curiosity and creativity among 8th to 12th-grade students through workshops, seminars, and competitions.


The Amateur Manager focuses on cultivating managerial skills and leadership among grades 11 and 12 students through real-world business challenges.

Our Timeline

2013

Inaugural edition of The Amateur Scientist (TAS)

2014

TAS grows in prominence, attracting national attention

2015

N. R. Narayana Murthy graces TAS with his presence

2016

Continued success and expansion of TAS

2017

TAS establishes itself as a premier national-level science event

2018

TAS continues to inspire and innovate

2019

Marking six successful years of TAS

2023

Inaugural edition of The Amateur Manager (TAM)

2024

The Amateur Scientist and The Amateur Manager join hands to form TAMS
